Rulin Chen (陈儒林)

Ph.D. Candidate

Singapore University of Technology and Design

Biography

Rulin Chen is currently a Ph.D. candidate at Computational Robotics Lab (CRL), SUTD, Singapore. He finished his Bachelor degree at Shantou University, China. The topic of his study mainly focuses on designing 3D assemblies using assembly-aware computational methods. Particularly, his work can be applied to design high-level interlocking puzzles.
