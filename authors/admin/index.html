Ziqi Wang (汪子琦)

Postdoctoral Researcher

ETH Zurich

Ziqi Wang is currently a Postdoctoral Reaseacher at Computational Robotics Lab (CRL), ETH Zurich. He finished his Ph.D. degree at Geometric Computing Laboratory (GCM), EPFL, supervised by Prof. Mark Pauly. The topic of his study mainly focuses on designing 3D assemblies using computational methods. Particularly, his work can be applied to design 3D interlocking puzzles, topological interlocking assemblies.

Ziqi was also a Ph.D. researcher at NCCR Digital Fabrication where he developed computational methods for Spatial Timber Assemblies and Large-Span Self-Supporting Assemblies.
